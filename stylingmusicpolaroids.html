Music Listening Data Polaroids

Prompt: Focus on something you do everyday and visualize that data

I chose music as my data and recorded my habits for two weeks. I then focused on the top 3 most listened to songs within the 3 genres as I tend to listen to the same songs on repeat. Simply put, it is my listening habits for 3 songs from alternative, pop, and rock compared. The variables I focused on in each genre were: minutes listened, mood, and activity I was engaged in while listening.I used photos I have taken to express the energy of each genre.
