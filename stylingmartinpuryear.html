Martin Puryear Art Book

Prompt: Design an art book showcasing artist Martin Puryears work + provided introductory text

Martin Puryear works with natural materials which influenced the color palette used. His work, to me, encapsulates the theme of freedom and containment so I wanted to represent that by following a grid but switching up the column and height width, so most of the content conforms to the grid in the containment sense but there are instances where image and shape break free of that grid.
